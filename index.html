About IDAYRIDE 2017

A Bikers Parade On Independence Day

Independence day Marks a very day for India, on this day back in 1947 india saw its first light of freedom from the british rule. To mark this the government honors those freedom fighters with a Parade performed by various Armed Forces Departments.This day we at UnitedKTM and other riding clubs have decided to honor those freedom fighters by making a Bikers Parade.So bikers out there please come over join the brotherhood and lead the honorary parade
